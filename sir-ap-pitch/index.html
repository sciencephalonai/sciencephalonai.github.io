Executive Summary

Deletions can decide 50+ seats. The window to act is now.

The Mechanism

How silent deletion works

BLOs visit 46,165 booths. Absent households — migrants, fishermen, seasonal workers — get marked "not traceable." Form 7 bulk objections are filed against valid voters. Names vanish in the draft roll before anyone notices.

The Statutory Window

Claims & objections are the only remedy

After the draft roll publishes, a statutory window allows Form 6 filings and Form 7 contests. Miss that window and the deletion is permanent. The platform exists to catch every case in time.

Prior AP Evidence — ECI Complaint Aug 28, 2023

40,000 gone in Vizag East. 23,000 in Vijayawada Central.

These deletions happened before SIR 2026. SIR is a larger, nationwide exercise with broader scope and ECI institutional backing. The 2026 exposure is materially higher.
